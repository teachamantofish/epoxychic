What is Epoxy?

High quality epoxys deliver a rugged scratch and heat resistant surface that is in many ways superior to Corian and stone. Not only is it less expensive and faster to install, it offers unlimited possibilities for color, design, and style. Why not get an imported stone look for 1/2 the price?

EpoxyChic does not use materials from local box stores, but rather sources quality materials to your specific job.
